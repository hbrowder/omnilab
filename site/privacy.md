# Privacy policy

**OmniLab.io** does not use cookies, does not embed third-party scripts, and does not collect personal information from visitors to this marketing site. There is no analytics provider, no advertising network, no fingerprinting, and no third-party content delivery.

When you download the OmniLab software (the `.deb` package or its sources from GitHub):

- The software runs entirely on hardware you control.
- It does **not** phone home, send telemetry, or report usage statistics back to OmniLab or any third party.
- The optional auto-update check (which you can disable in the UI) queries `https://github.com/hbrowder/omnilab/releases` over HTTPS to see if a newer version is published. No identifying information is sent — it's a plain HTTPS GET request.
- The optional license activation flow contacts our payment processor (Stripe) to verify your subscription. Stripe's privacy policy applies to that interaction; OmniLab itself does not store payment information.

If you contact us by email, we'll use your email address to reply and nothing else. We don't add it to a mailing list, and we don't share it.
